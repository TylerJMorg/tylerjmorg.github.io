I'm Tyler! I'm a classical double bassist in northern Utah with a deep passion for classical european and cultural music. I got the privilege to perform for some wonderful and talented folks like Andrea Bocelli and Loren Allred in the Delta Center (a.k.a. the Vivint Arena) back in 2021.

Have loved technology all my life. I have extensive interest in preventative cybersecurity and advocate for emerging technologies such as WebAuthn, Device Bound Session Credentials (DBSC), and Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE).

I'm a big fan of writing technical papers. You can view my latest work, the Document Organization System (DOS) on GitHub or view the latest PDF.

DMs and email are open; feel free to get in touch!
